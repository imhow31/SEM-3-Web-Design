FAQ

(Frequently Asked Question)

-----------------------------

  1. Should I necessary register as a member for order?

No, you may make purchase as guest. However, we suggest to register as member with any ways that provided. Hence, you may trace your purchase history better.

  1. How to pay?

For order manually via Facebook Messenger, you may bank transfer to our company account.

  1. How much for postage fee?

The postage is RM5 for whole Malaysia.

  1. Can I cancel my order?

You may contact us via Facebook Messenger or any contact we provided and provide your order ID.
We will try our best to meet your requirement but order unable cancel once it has been processed.

  1. What should I do if I missed out he delivery?

You may contact courier company by providing your tracking number for requesting them to arrange resend.
Take note that we will NOT arrange parcel resend once the parcel was return to the sender address.
