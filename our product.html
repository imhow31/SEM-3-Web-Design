Detail

Clothing increasingly emphasizes details, and any processing of details will be reflected on the machine. For example, the stitching of some big-name clothes abroad such as AMARNY and other clothes is very clean. Once the line came over to cover all the dark lines, there was no dark line at all. Even the roughness that the lining is no longer processed tends to be flat, leaving little stitches. This trend will inevitably make those sewing machines that can meet the processing requirements very popular. Any slight changes in the brightness of the stitches, the size of the spacing, the thickness of the fabric, the splicing of different fabrics, etc. will deduce different fashions and styles, and accordingly will require different models. Therefore, it is of great significance to strengthen the communication with the printing master for the production orientation of sewing equipment enterprises.
